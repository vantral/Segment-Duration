The Movima DoReCo dataset was compiled by Katharina Haude between 2003 and 2008 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Celia Birle, Alejandra Camelo Cruz, Michelle Throssell, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Movima DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Katharina Haude's Movima data that is archived at TLA (https://hdl.handle.net/1839/963ce6c7-f17c-466b-9f3e-aec007ea0659).

Movima DoReCo annotation files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

Movima DoReCo audio files are not available in DoReCo, but they are accessible at TLA https://hdl.handle.net/1839/963ce6c7-f17c-466b-9f3e-aec007ea0659

The Movima DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Haude, Katharina. 2022. Movima DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.da42xf67).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
