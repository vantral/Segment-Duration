The Hoocąk DoReCo dataset was compiled by Iren Hartmann between 2003 and 2012 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Karl Oßwald, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Hoocąk DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Iren Hartmann's Hoocąk data that is archived at TLA (https://hdl.handle.net/1839/bf362b19-2cd6-4bee-af44-a46446077875).

Hoocąk DoReCo annotation files are distributed under an Attribution-NonComercial-NoDerivs 2.0 Generic (CC BY-NC-ND 2.0) License (https://www.creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/2.0/).

Hoocąk DoReCo audio files are not available in DoReCo, but they are accessible at TLA https://hdl.handle.net/1839/bf362b19-2cd6-4bee-af44-a46446077875

The Hoocąk DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Hartmann, Iren. 2022. Hoocąk DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.b57f5065).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
