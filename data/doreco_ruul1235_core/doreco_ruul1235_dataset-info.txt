The Ruuli DoReCo dataset was compiled by Saudah Namyalo, Alena Witzlack-Makarevich, Zarina Molochieva, and Anatol Kiriggwajjo between 2012 and 2019 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Ruuli DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Saudah Namyalo, Alena Witzlack-Makarevich, Zarina Molochieva, and Anatol Kiriggwajjo's Ruuli data.
