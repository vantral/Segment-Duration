The Cabécar DoReCo dataset was compiled by Juan Diego Quesada, Stavros Skopeteas, Carolina Pasamonik, Carolin Brokmann, and Florian Fischer in 2011 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Alejandra Camelo Cruz, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Cabécar DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Juan Diego Quesada, Stavros Skopeteas, Carolina Pasamonik, Carolin Brokmann, and Florian Fischer's Cabécar data that is archived at TLA (https://hdl.handle.net/1839/00-0000-0000-0021-E375-1).
