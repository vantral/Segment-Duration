The French (Swiss) DoReCo dataset was compiled by Mathieu Avanzi, Marie-José Béguelin, Gilles Corminboeuf, Federica Diémoz, and Laure Anne Johnsen between 2014 and 2015 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Hannah Ida Hullmeine, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the French (Swiss) DoReCo dataset are based on are part of the following, larger collection:
Avanzi, Mathieu, Marie-José Béguelin, Gilles Corminboeuf, Federica Diémoz & Laure Anne Johnsen. 2012-2022. Corpus OFROM – corpus oral de français de Suisse romande. Université de Neuchâtel. http://www.unine.ch/ofrom.

The French (Swiss) DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Avanzi, Mathieu , Marie-José Béguelin, Gilles Corminboeuf, Federica Diémoz and Laure Anne Johnsen. 2022. French (Swiss) DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.3520l685).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
