The Anal DoReCo dataset was compiled by Pavel Ozerov between 2015 and 2022, supervising a team of Anal Naga transcribers and translators (approved by ANTA and ALS). The data were further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Xia Qiang, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Anal DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Pavel Ozerov's Anal data that is archived at ELAR (http://hdl.handle.net/2196/00-0000-0000-000F-CB55-6).
