The Yurakaré DoReCo dataset was compiled by Sonja Gipper and Jeremías Ballivián Torrico in 2013 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Alejandra Camelo Cruz, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Yurakaré DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Sonja Gipper and Jeremías Ballivián Torrico's Yurakaré data that is archived at LAC (https://lac.uni-koeln.de/collection/11341/00-0000-0000-0000-1BDA-F).

Yurakaré DoReCo annotation files are distributed under an Attribution-NonComercial-NoDerivs 2.0 Generic (CC BY-NC-ND 2.0) License (https://www.creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/2.0/).

Yurakaré DoReCo audio files are not available in DoReCo, but they are accessible at LAC https://lac.uni-koeln.de/collection/11341/00-0000-0000-0000-1BDA-F

The Yurakaré DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Gipper, Sonja and Jeremías Ballivián Torrico. 2022. Yurakaré DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.7ca412wg).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
