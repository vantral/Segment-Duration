The Dolgan DoReCo dataset was compiled by Chris Lasse Däbritz, Nina Kudryakova, Eugénie Stapert, and Alexandre Arkhipov based on recordings created between 1972 and 2010 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Elena Lazarenko, Johanna Kimmerl, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Dolgan DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Chris Lasse Däbritz, Nina Kudryakova, Eugénie Stapert, and Alexandre Arkhipov's Dolgan data that is archived at HZSK (http://hdl.handle.net/11022/0000-0007-CAE7-1).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
