The Baïnounk Gubëeher DoReCo dataset was compiled by Alexander Yao Cobbinah between 2009 and 2014 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Laura Günther, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Baïnounk Gubëeher DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Alexander Yao Cobbinah's Baïnounk Gubëeher data that is archived at ELAR (http://hdl.handle.net/2196/00-0000-0000-000E-38AC-3).

Baïnounk Gubëeher DoReCo audio files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

The Baïnounk Gubëeher DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Cobbinah, Alexander Yao. 2022. Baïnounk Gubëeher DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.a332abw8).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
