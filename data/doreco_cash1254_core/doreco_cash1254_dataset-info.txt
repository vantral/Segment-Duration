The Cashinahua DoReCo dataset was compiled by Sabine Reiter in 2006 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Emma Ritz, Frederic Blum, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Cashinahua DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Sabine Reiter's Cashinahua data that is archived at TLA (https://hdl.handle.net/1839/00-0000-0000-0008-4070-6).

Cashinahua DoReCo audio files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

The Cashinahua DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Reiter, Sabine. 2022. Cashinahua DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.a8f9q2f1).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
