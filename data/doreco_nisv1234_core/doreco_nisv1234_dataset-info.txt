The Nisvai DoReCo dataset was compiled by Jocelyn Aznar between 2011 and 2015 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Johanna Kimmerl, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. A larger collection of Jocelyn Aznar's Nisvai data is archived at Ortolang (https://hdl.handle.net/11403/sldr000783/v2).

The Nisvai DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Aznar, Jocelyn. 2022. Nisvai DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.2801565f).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
