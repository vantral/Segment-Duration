The Komnzo DoReCo dataset was compiled by Christian Döhler between 2010 and 2015 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Alejandra Camelo Cruz, Nora Hofmann, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Komnzo DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Christian Döhler's Komnzo data that is archived at Zenodo (https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.4695271).

Komnzo DoReCo audio files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

The Komnzo DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Döhler, Christian. 2022. Komnzo DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.c5e6dudv).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
