The Lower Sorbian DoReCo dataset was compiled by Hauke Bartels and Marcin Szczepański between 2011 and 2013 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Lower Sorbian DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Hauke Bartels and Marcin Szczepański's Lower Sorbian data that is archived at TLA (https://hdl.handle.net/1839/b7cb7b41-8362-4488-b8cb-86fbfea6c7c7).

Lower Sorbian DoReCo annotation files are distributed under an Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/).

Lower Sorbian DoReCo audio files are not available in DoReCo, but they are accessible at TLA https://hdl.handle.net/1839/b7cb7b41-8362-4488-b8cb-86fbfea6c7c7

The Lower Sorbian DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Bartels, Hauke and Marcin Szczepański. 2022. Lower Sorbian DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.6c6e4e9k).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
