The Kakabe DoReCo dataset was compiled by Alexandra Vydrina between 2013 and 2015 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Stephan Lünser, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Kakabe DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Alexandra Vydrina's Kakabe data that is archived at ELAR (http://hdl.handle.net/2196/00-0000-0000-0000-A924-0).

Kakabe DoReCo annotation files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

Kakabe DoReCo audio files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

The Kakabe DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Vydrina, Alexandra. 2022. Kakabe DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.d5aeu9t6).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
