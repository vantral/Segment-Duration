The Warlpiri DoReCo dataset was compiled by Carmel O'Shannessy between 2004 and 2010 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Frederic Blum, Michelle Elizabeth Throssell Balagué, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. Transcriptions for the original dataset were created with the help of Emma Browne, Fiona Gibson, Tanya Hargraves, Dion Long, Kayla Long and Gretel Macdonald. The files that the Warlpiri DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Carmel O'Shannessy's Warlpiri data that is archived at TLA (http://hdl.handle.net/1839/bbfb5fb0-e24b-47a3-9690-3f2d5a61ad42).

The Warlpiri DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
O'Shannessy, Carmel. 2022. Warlpiri DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.042dv614).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
