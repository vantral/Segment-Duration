The Nǁng DoReCo dataset was compiled by Tom Güldemann, Martina Ernszt, Sven Siegmund, and Alena Witzlack-Makarevich between 2004 and 2009 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Nǁng DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Tom Güldemann, Martina Ernszt, Sven Siegmund, and Alena Witzlack-Makarevich's Nǁng data.
