The Tabaq (Karko) DoReCo dataset was compiled by Birgit Hellwig, Gertrud Schneider-Blum, and Khaleel Bakheet Khaleel Ismail between 2011 and 2013 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Nora Hofmann, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Tabaq (Karko) DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Birgit Hellwig, Gertrud Schneider-Blum, and Khaleel Bakheet Khaleel Ismail's Tabaq (Karko) data that is archived at ELAR (https://hdl.handle.net/2196/00-0000-0000-0002-2EAA-F).

Tabaq (Karko) DoReCo annotation files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

Tabaq (Karko) DoReCo audio files are not available in DoReCo, but they are accessible at ELAR http://hdl.handle.net/2196/00-0000-0000-0002-2EAA-F
