The Northern Kurdish (Kurmanji) DoReCo dataset was compiled by Geoff Haig, Maria Vollmer, and Hanna Thiele between 2000 and 2004 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Frederic Blum, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The Northern Kurdish (Kurmanji) DoReCo dataset with an "mc_" prefix are from Geoff Haig, Maria Vollmer, and Hanna Thiele's Northern Kurdish (Kurmanji) Multi-CAST dataset (https://multicast.aspra.uni-bamberg.de/#nkurd), and these are additionally annotated with the GRAID (Grammatical Relations and Animacy in Discourse) and RefIND (Referent Indexing in Natural Language Discourse) annotation schemes (see https://multicast.aspra.uni-bamberg.de).
