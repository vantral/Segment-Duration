The Savosavo DoReCo dataset was compiled by Claudia Wegener between 2003 and 2008 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Francie Höhler, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Savosavo DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Claudia Wegener's Savosavo data that is archived at TLA (https://hdl.handle.net/1839/00-0000-0000-000D-B694-5).

Savosavo DoReCo audio files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

The Savosavo DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Wegener, Claudia. 2022. Savosavo DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.b74d1b33).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
