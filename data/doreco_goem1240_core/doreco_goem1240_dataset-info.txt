The Goemai DoReCo dataset was compiled by Birgit Hellwig between 1999 and 2000 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Xia Qiang, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Goemai DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Birgit Hellwig's Goemai data that is archived at TLA (https://hdl.handle.net/1839/00-0000-0000-0000-6B5E-B).

Goemai DoReCo annotation files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

Goemai DoReCo audio files are not available in DoReCo, but they are accessible at TLA https://hdl.handle.net/1839/00-0000-0000-0000-6B5E-B

The Goemai DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Hellwig, Birgit. 2022. Goemai DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Universit√© Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.b93664ml).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
