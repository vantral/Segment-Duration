The Mojeño Trinitario DoReCo dataset was compiled by Françoise Rose between 2005 and 2018 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Emma Ritz, Ludger Paschen, and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Mojeño Trinitario DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Françoise Rose's Mojeño Trinitario data.

Mojeño Trinitario DoReCo annotation files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

Mojeño Trinitario DoReCo audio files are distributed under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

The Mojeño Trinitario DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Rose, Françoise. 2022. Mojeño Trinitario DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.cbc3b4xr).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.

Please note also that annotations of this corpus are well advanced but not necessarily definitive and that people interested in working with the data/citing the data are encouraged to contact Françoise Rose to obtain updated annotations.
