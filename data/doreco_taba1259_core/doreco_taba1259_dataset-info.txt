The Tabasaran DoReCo dataset was compiled by Natalia Bogomolova, Dmitry Ganenkov, and Nils Norman Schiborr in 2010 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Tabasaran DoReCo dataset are based on are from Natalia Bogomolova, Dmitry Ganenkov, and Nils Norman Schiborr's Tabasaran Multi-CAST dataset (https://multicast.aspra.uni-bamberg.de/#tabasaran) and they are additionally annotated with the GRAID (Grammatical Relations and Animacy in Discourse) and RefIND (Referent Indexing in Natural Language Discourse) annotation schemes (see https://multicast.aspra.uni-bamberg.de).
