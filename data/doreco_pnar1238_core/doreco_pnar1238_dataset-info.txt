The Pnar DoReCo dataset was compiled by Hiram Ring between 2011 and 2012 and further processed by the DoReCo team (in particular Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave) between 2019 and 2022. The files that the Pnar DoReCo dataset are based on are part of a larger collection of Hiram Ring's Pnar data that is archived at NTU (doi.org/10.21979/N9/KVFGBZ).

Pnar DoReCo annotation files are distributed under an Attribution-NonComercial 2.0 Generic (CC BY-NC 2.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/2.0/).

Pnar DoReCo audio files are distributed under an Attribution-NonComercial 2.0 Generic (CC BY-NC 2.0) License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/2.0/).

The Pnar DoReCo dataset should be cited as follows:
Ring, Hiram. 2022. Pnar DoReCo dataset. In Seifart, Frank, Ludger Paschen and Matthew Stave (eds.). Language Documentation Reference Corpus (DoReCo) 1.2. Berlin & Lyon: Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft & laboratoire Dynamique Du Langage (UMR5596, CNRS & Université Lyon 2). (https://doi.org/10.34847/nkl.5ba1062k).

Please note that when citing this dataset, or any number of DoReCo datasets, it is NOT sufficient to refer to DoReCo as a whole, but the full citation for each individual dataset must be provided, including the name(s) of the creator(s) of each data set.

A web interface for easy access to this dataset as well as other DoReCo datasets and further information can be found at https://doreco.huma-num.fr.
